
A mini game for our class. Nothing special

Singleton, Observer and a mini version of MVC are used.  
Pooling system is used for zombies, foods, targets and ghost.  
2 VFX graphs (loot drop and portal) and 2 shader graphs (candle flame and dissolve) are used.  
2 different post processing (death and life) are used. Use portal to change one another.
